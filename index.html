About Us

Shiningyear Fabric LTD is committed to delivering quality fabrics with a customer-centric approach and a focus on sustainability.

Established in 1982, Shiningyear Fabric LTD has been a trusted leader in the industrial textile field for over 43 years. We specialize in high-quality coated fabrics, including waterproof industrial textiles, leisure and sports fabrics, rocksack materials, tent and raincoat fabrics, umbrella cloth, and more.

Our product range features materials such as Nylon, Polyester, Dobby, Printing, and stocklots, all enhanced by advanced technical processing services including PVC coating, PU coating, and water resistance treatments. Exporting worldwide, we proudly serve a diverse clientele—from kite manufacturers to industrial bag makers—adapting to the ever-changing textile market with flexibility and innovation.

Our reputation is built on listening to our customers, maintaining an extensive stock of coated fabrics, and delivering exceptional customer service. At Shiningyear Fabric LTD, we are dedicated to providing reliable textile solutions, outstanding product quality, and a commitment to excellence that our clients can depend on.
