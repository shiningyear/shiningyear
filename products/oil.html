Oil Coating

Product Description

Oil-coated fabrics are mainly used for waterproofing and durability. They are commonly used in outdoor gear like raincoats and tents, protective coverings, medical textiles, automotive applications, and industrial uses that require water and stain resistance along with toughness. This coating helps fabrics resist moisture, stains, and abrasion, making them ideal for harsh environments.

Specifications

  • Material: Nylon Nylon or Polyester
  • Denier: 70D、210D、420D、600D、840D、1680D (commonly available)
  • Width: 150 cm
  • Weight: 130–360 gsm (depending on denier and weave)
