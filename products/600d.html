600D Polyester

Product Description

600D Polyester is a highly durable and cost-effective fabric commonly used for backpacks, luggage, and outdoor products. It offers reliable resistance to tearing, UV damage, and environmental wear. This fabric is easily treated with coatings to improve water repellency and surface durability.

  • Technical treatments: PVC/PU coating, water resistance available
