Dobby

Product Description

Dobby fabric is a type of woven fabric known for its textured surface and durability, making it a popular choice for bags. It's created using a dobby loom, which allows for the weaving of small, geometric patterns into the fabric. This construction not only adds visual appeal but also enhances the fabric's strength and resistance to tearing and abrasion, making it well-suited for bags, especially those used for outdoor activities or requiring high durability.

Specifications

  • Material: Nylon or Polyester (Dobby weave)
  • Denier: 70D, 210D, 420D, 840D（commonly available）
  • Width: 150 cm
  • Weight: 130–360 gsm (depending on denier and weave)
  • Technical treatments: PVC/PU coating, water resistance available
