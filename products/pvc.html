PVC Coating

Product Description

PVC coating gives fabric high waterproofing, abrasion resistance, and durability, while also protecting against stains, UV rays, and chemicals. PVC-coated fabrics are commonly used in products like luggage, tents, rain gear, protective covers, and awnings. Due to their toughness, waterproof nature, and weather resistance, they are suitable for various industrial and outdoor applications

Specifications

  • Material: Nylon or Polyester
  • Denier: 70D、210D、420D、600D、840D、1680D (commonly available)
  • Width: 150 cm
  • Weight: 130–360 gsm (depending on denier and weave)
