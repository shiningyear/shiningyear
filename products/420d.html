420D Nylon

Product Description

420D Nylon is a durable, medium-weight fabric designed for travel gear, sport equipment, and everyday use. It delivers superior tear and abrasion resistance while maintaining flexibility and resilience. Available with PU or PVC coatings, it provides enhanced water resistance and long-lasting performance in varied environments.

Specifications

  • Material: Nylon
  • Denier: 420D
  • Width: 150 cm
  • Weight: 190-230 gsm
  • Technical treatments: PVC/PU coating, water resistance available
