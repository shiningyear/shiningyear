70D Nylon

Product Description

70D Nylon is a lightweight and durable fabric prized for its softness and breathability. Commonly used in linings, lightweight bags, and apparel, it offers good strength while maintaining flexibility. This fabric can be treated with various coatings to enhance water resistance and durability, making it suitable for less demanding yet versatile applications.

Specifications

  • Material: Nylon
  • Denier: 70D
  • Width: 150 cm
  • Weight: 60-70 gsm
  • Technical treatments: PVC/PU coating, water resistance available
