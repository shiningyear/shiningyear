Foam Coating

Product Description

Foam coating creates tiny bubbles by adding a foaming agent, which expands through heating or pressure, making the material lightweight and providing good elasticity, thermal insulation, soundproofing, and cushioning properties. Foam coating is commonly used in furniture, car seats, packaging materials, sports equipment, and insulation products, enhancing comfort while improving protective performance.

Specifications

  • Material: Nylon or Polyester
  • Denier: 70D、210D、420D、600D、840D、1680D (commonly available)
  • Width: 150 cm
  • Weight: 130–360 gsm (depending on denier and weave)
