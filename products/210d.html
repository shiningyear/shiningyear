210D Nylon

Product Description

210D Nylon is a versatile and reliable fabric valued for its balance of lightweight and strength. Commonly used in backpacks, inner compartments, and outdoor gear, it offers excellent abrasion resistance and durability while remaining flexible. This fabric is available with various coatings to enhance water repellence and performance for demanding applications.

Specifications

  • Material: Nylon
  • Denier: 210D
  • Width: 150 cm
  • Weight: 125-193 gsm
  • Technical treatments: PVC/PU coating, water resistance available
