840D Nylon

Product Description

840D Nylon is a strong and rugged fabric engineered for heavy-duty applications such as industrial bags, outdoor covers, and tactical gear. Its dense weave resists abrasion and wear, ensuring durability under tough conditions. Coating options improve its water repellency and weather resistance for extended use.

Specifications

  • Material: Nylon

  • Width: 150 cm
  • Weight: 230-270 gsm
  • Technical treatments: PVC/PU coating, water resistance available
