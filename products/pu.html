PU Coating

Product Description

PU coating (polyurethane coating) is a thin, flexible layer applied to fabric to make it water-resistant, more durable, and resistant to abrasion. It’s commonly used in outdoor gear, bags, and jackets to improve protection and longevity without adding much weight.

Specifications

  • Material: Nylon or Polyester
  • Denier: 70D、210D、420D、600D、840D、1680D (commonly available)
  • Width: 150 cm
  • Weight: 130–360 gsm (depending on denier and weave)
